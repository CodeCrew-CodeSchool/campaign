About SmartEmailer

SmartEmailer was created to make sending personalized marketing emails fast, simple, and effective. Our platform empowers businesses, creators, and entrepreneurs to reach their audience with impactful, well-crafted emails without the hassle of manual drafting. Whether you’re running a small business or managing a large campaign, SmartEmailer helps you save time while maximizing engagement.

Our Story

SmartEmailer is the brainchild of Bruce Brown and Kadarius Clemons. Both of us attended Code Crew's Code School, where we learned the skills to turn ideas into real software. Inspired by the challenge of automating marketing and making email outreach more accessible, we combined our coding knowledge and creativity to build SmartEmailer.

Why We Built SmartEmailer

We believe that technology should simplify your work, not complicate it. SmartEmailer was designed to give you more time to focus on what matters—growing your business, connecting with your customers, and building relationships. Our goal is to empower anyone, regardless of technical skill, to create meaningful, personalized email campaigns with confidence.

Our Mission

At SmartEmailer, our mission is to provide an easy-to-use, reliable, and effective tool for marketing communication. We want every user to feel empowered and inspired by the emails they send, knowing that each message represents their brand and voice perfectly.

“SmartEmailer is more than just a tool. It’s a bridge between your ideas and your audience.”
