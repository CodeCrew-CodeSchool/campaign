How SmartEmailer Works

SmartEmailer lets you create personalized marketing emails and send them efficiently to your recipients.

  1. Describe Your Product or Service: Input the details of what you are promoting.
  2. Choose Email Volume: Select the number of emails you want to send.
  3. Upload Recipients: Paste email addresses or upload a CSV file.
  4. Customize Your Email: Add sender info, subject lines, and personalize for each recipient.
  5. Generate Emails: SmartEmailer automatically generates professional marketing emails for your campaign.
